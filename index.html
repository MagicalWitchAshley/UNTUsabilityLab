Welcome!

The University of North Texas’ Usability Lab is focused on providing a space for collaborative research, ability for experiential learning, and encouraging educational resources for usability and user experience in all aspects. By providing the space and technology, the usability lab hopes to enhance the learning experience of UX professionals. Since the university provides courses that focus on user experience and usability, the usability lab is designed to expand that information to better cater to the growing professional field of usability and user experience design.

Why a Usability Lab?

Usability labs help with the process of usability testing, which is an integral part of the usability and user experience education and profession. Usability labs provide a profound environment that is solely focused on the expansion and redefining of usability and user experience methods. Our usability lab fits with the goals of the university, to provide an innovative scholarly experience that prepares our students for their intended careers. The unique advantage of our usability lab is that it provides a chance for experience and innovation to our students, faculty, staff, and clients in the UX profession.

Usability and User Experience

Usability and user experience are essential to the successful implementation platforms and their users. Usability focuses on the design of the website and its ability to be used successfully, while user experience focuses on the overall interaction of the user of the website. For the usability lab at UNT, in correlation with the education offered, provides the impact of putting usability and user experience into research and projects creates a platform for better understanding.
